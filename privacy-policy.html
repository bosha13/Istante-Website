Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Istante is a minimalist stopwatch app for iOS. The app does not require an account, login, or any special configuration.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, sell, or share users' personal information, including children's personal information.

Istante does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers. The app does not track user activity across other apps or websites.

Local Data

Istante may store app settings and the current timer state locally on the user's device so that the app works correctly between launches. This data remains on the user's device and is not sent to us.
